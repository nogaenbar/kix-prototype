Browser needs hard refresh to see button changes.

Try one of these:

1. **Hard Refresh (Recommended):**
   - Mac: Cmd + Shift + R
   - Windows/Linux: Ctrl + Shift + R

2. **Clear Cache & Reload:**
   - Mac: Cmd + Option + R
   - Windows: Ctrl + F5

3. **Manual Refresh:**
   - Close browser tab
   - Reopen http://localhost:3000
